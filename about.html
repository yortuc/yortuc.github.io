About

Short bio

I'm a software engineer focused on building AI systems that behave reliably in real-world settings. My work sits at the intersection of agentic AI, system design, and product delivery, with a strong emphasis on evaluation, observability, and safety.

What I care about

  • Safe and controllable autonomy
  • Measuring AI behavior, not just outputs
  • Evaluation as a continuous lifecycle, not a one-time test
  • Turning product goals into concrete system signals

How I work

I enjoy working on systems where engineering and product thinking intersect. I often help define what “good” looks like for AI features and translate that into evaluation pipelines, datasets, and automated checks that support safe iteration.

Current focus

  • Agent evaluation methodologies
  • Scenario-based simulation for AI systems
  • Observability and reliability for multi-step AI workflows
